## **üìò Quest System Overview**

The quest system allows players to complete objectives across the world for rewards. Quests range from simple tasks like gathering items to in-depth story chains that reveal world lore.

#### **How It Works**

* Use **`/quests`** to open the Quest Menu.
* Choose a category to browse available quests.
* Complete quests to receive a quest key!
* Daily and Weekly quests reset automatically.
