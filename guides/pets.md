# Pets

Our pets allow you to bring adorable virtual pets into your Minecraft family. If you're looking to add some companionship and cuteness to your gaming experience, one of these pets is the perfect option for you!&#x20;

Once purchased, your new friend can be accessed through via /pets





#### **Random pet info**

If another player kills your pet - dont worry! You can just spawn him in again.
