# Navigating Chat/Commands









Wish you could chat in different channels? You now can!

* (/) ch
  * Able to click and enable various other channels
    * Can only type to one at a time
      * can do command to send to another chat without changing.
* /gc
  * Sets you to write to the global channel
* /gc {message}
  * Sends a single message to the global channel while maintaining your current channel selection
* /tc
  * Sets you to write to your town channel
* /tc {message}
  * Sends a single message to your town channel while maintaining your current channel selection
* /nc
  * Sets you to write to your nation channel
* /nc {message}
  * Sends a single message to your nation channel while maintaining your current channel selection
* /lc
  * Sets you to write to the local channel
* /lc {message}
  * Sends a single message to the local channel while maintaining your current channel selection
