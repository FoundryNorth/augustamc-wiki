# Marriage!

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/marriagemaster.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Marriage Master allows you to marry another player in Minecraft to gain some extras.</p></figcaption></figure>

* Shared Home with partner
* Teleport to partner
* XP boost when near partner (for Minecraft XP, mcMMO and SkillAPI)
  * Married Players down receive a 1.5xp McMMMO boost when near eachother.&#x20;
  * Married Players down recieve a 1.5xp XP boost when near eachother.&#x20;
* Faster health regeneration when near partner
* Private Chat with partner
* Marry players through a priest or on your own
* Enable/Disable PvP with your partner
* Ability to kiss your partner (with particle effect)
* Gift Items to your partner
* Option to enable the need to accept the marriage
* [Minepacks](https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/19286/) integration to share backpacks

Marriage -> Costs 10k - Each player pays half.&#x20;

Divorce -> Costs 1k - Each plaper pays half.&#x20;



Married players should now have this full permission to the following commands.



* /marry - Marries two players, only for priests&#x20;
* /marry divorce - Divorces two players, only for priests&#x20;
* /marry list - Shows all married players
* &#x20;/marry tp - Teleports to the partner&#x20;
* /marry sethome - Sets the home for two married players&#x20;
* /marry home - Teleports to the home of married players&#x20;
* /marry chat - Allows to chat a couple private
* /marry chat toggle - Toggles the chat to send all chat messages to the partner instead of to the public&#x20;
* /marry pvpon - Turns PvP between two married players on&#x20;
* /marry pvpoff - Turns PvP between two married players off&#x20;
* /marry kiss - To kiss your partner&#x20;
* /marry gift - Gifts the item in your hand to your partner
