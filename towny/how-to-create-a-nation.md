Nations are the next step above towns, offering large-scale collaboration.

#### **Creating a Nation**

Requirements:

* Must be a mayor of a qualifying town
* Enough money in your town bank
* Command:
  * /nation new

#### **Nation Benefits**

* Bigger influence on the server
* Shared chat
* Nation-wide spawn (optional)
* Unified alliances
* Potential bonuses depending on server settings

#### **Nation Ranks**

Assign roles to mayors or trusted members:

* King
* Chancellor
* General
* Officer

Command:

* /nation rank add \<player> \<rank><br>
