## **üìÑ What is Towny?**

**Towny** is a community-based land management system that lets players create towns, form nations, and grow thriving communities in a protected, player-driven world. It allows players to claim land, prevent griefing, collaborate on builds, and develop long-term settlements.

***

#### **Core Features**

* **Towns:** Create or join a community with shared land and protection.
* **Mayors & Residents:** Towns are led by mayors and managed by assigned ranks.
* **Plots:** Land is divided into claimable chunks you can assign for different uses.
* **Nations:** Large clusters of towns form powerful nations.
* **Protection:** Prevent griefing using Towny‚Äôs area protections and plot permissions.
* **Economy:** Taxes, upkeep, and bank balances allow towns to grow strategically.
